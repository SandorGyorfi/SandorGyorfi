About Me

Combining technology and creativity to build impactful web solutions.

Hello there! I'm Sandor, a 39-year-old web developer based in the vibrant city of Bristol, UK. Last year, I took a significant leap in my career by earning a Diploma in Web Application Development, rounding off over a decade of business experience with some serious coding skills.

My journey into web development wasn't just about adding another feather to my cap; it was about turning my passion for technology into tangible skills that can create, innovate, and solve problems. Whether it's spinning up a small personal site or engineering the backend of enterprise applications, I'm all in.

I thrive on collaboration and love being part of the dynamic, ever-evolving tech community on social media. It's where I share, learn, and connect with like-minded folks who are just as excited about the potential of the web as I am.

But life isn't all code and deploy. When I'm not in front of a computer screen, you'll probably find me exploring new destinations or getting lost in music. Traveling and music aren't just hobbies; they're my sources of inspiration and creativity that I bring back to my work.

Over the years, I've learned that web development is more than just building websites; it's about crafting experiences that matter, that speak to people. And that's exactly what I aim to do with every line of code I write.

I'm looking forward to working on our next project together.
